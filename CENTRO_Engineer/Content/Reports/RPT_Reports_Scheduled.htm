About scheduled reports

The Reports > Reports > Scheduled page allows you to schedule real-time reports that contain a detailed summary of the well and the drilling performance on the rig. Scheduled reports can be generated on an hourly, daily, or weekly basis.

  • Hourly – Hourly reports are generated for a custom frequency, up to 12 hours. See "Creating an hourly report".
  • Daily – Daily reports are generated every 24 hours, at the time specified in the Report Time field. See "Creating a daily report".
  • Weekly – Weekly reports are generated every seven days, on the day specified in the Day of Week field. See "Creating a weekly report".

When a scheduled report is generated, the report will be compressed into a zip file and emailed to the email addresses listed in the Recipients list. It will also be saved to the C:\ProgramData\Weatherford\OneSync\Monitoring\Reports directory on your computer.

Note

In order for CENTRO® Engineer to send emails, your email settings must be configured by your network administrator.

Note

If the compressed file size of the report exceeds 20 MB, the report will not be emailed. However, it will still be saved to the C:\ProgramData\Weatherford\OneSync\Monitoring\Reports directory.

Creating an hourly report

To generate an hourly real-time report:

  1. Go to the Reports > Reports > Scheduled page.
  2. Select the Hourly option.
  3. In the Frequency field, enter the number of hours that you want the report to cover.
  4. Select the information you want to include in the report. See "Report details".
  5. In the Recipients field, enter the email addresses that you want to send the report to. Multiple email addresses should be separated by a comma.
  6. Click the Save Changes button () at the top of the screen.

The first hourly report will be generated when the next interval begins. New reports will generated at the interval specified in the Frequency field. The reports will be automatically emailed to all email addresses listed in the Recipients field. When you open the attached report in MS Excel, click the Enable Content button to enable all of the report features. The report will also be saved to the C:\ProgramData\Weatherford\OneSync\Monitoring\Reports directory on your computer.

Creating a daily report

To generate a daily real-time report:

  1. Go to the Reports > Reports > Scheduled page.
  2. Select the Daily option.
  3. In the Report Time field, enter the time that you want the report to be generated.
  4. Select the information you want to include in the report. See "Report details".
  5. In the Recipients field, enter the email addresses that you want to send the report to. Multiple email addresses should be separated by a comma.
  6. Click the Save Changes button () at the top of the screen.

The first daily report will be generated at the specified time. New reports will be generated every 24 hours after the initial report. The reports will be automatically emailed to all email addresses listed in the Recipients field. When you open the attached report in MS Excel, click the Enable Content button to enable all of the report features. The report will also be saved to the C:\ProgramData\Weatherford\OneSync\Monitoring\Reports directory on your computer.

Creating a weekly report

To generate a weekly real-time report:

  1. Go to the Reports > Reports > Scheduled page.
  2. Select the Weekly option.
  3. In the Day of Week field, select the day that you want the first report to be generated.
  4. In the Report Time field, enter the time that you want the report to be generated.
  5. Select the information you want to include in the report. See "Report details".
  6. In the Recipients field, enter the email addresses that you want to send the report to. Multiple email addresses should be separated by a comma.
  7. Click the Save Changes button () at the top of the screen.

The first weekly report will be generated on the specified day, at the specified time. New reports will be generated every 7 days after the initial report. The reports will be automatically emailed to all email addresses listed in the Recipients field. When you open the attached report in MS Excel, click the Enable Content button to enable all of the report features. The report will also be saved to the C:\ProgramData\Weatherford\OneSync\Monitoring\Reports directory on your computer.
