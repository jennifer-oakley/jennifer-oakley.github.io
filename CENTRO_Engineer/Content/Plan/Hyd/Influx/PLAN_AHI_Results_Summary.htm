Influx summary

The results summary for an influx simulation includes several critical values calculated during the influx and a surface backpressure schedule.

Critical values for flow rates, pit volume, and pressure are shown on the right side of the screen. The simulation calculates the following critical values:

Calculated values for an influx analysis summary
Field Definition
Pore Pressure Pore pressure at the depth of interest, converted from density units into pressure units
Time To Reach RCD Time required to circulate the influx out of the well
Max Liquid Flow Out Maximum flow rate of the fluids exiting the well
Final Pump Flow Rate Pump flow rate at the end of the influx
Max Pit Volume Maximum fluid volume of the mud pit
Max Pressure @ Surface Maximum pressure found at the surface
Max Pressure @ CS Maximum pressure found at the casing shoe
Max Pressure @ DOI Maximum pressure found at the depth of interest
Max Pressure @ Bottom Maximum pressure found at the bottom of the well

The results summary also includes a surface backpressure schedule. The surface backpressure schedule is a time-based profile of the influx, calculated in one minute increments. For each minute of the influx, the simulation calculates the values listed in the table below. To view the surface backpressure schedule, click the Show Full Results button () at the top of the screen.

Calculated values for a surface backpressure schedule
Column Definition
Time Time elapsed since the influx began
Lead MD MD at the top of the influx
Tail MD MD at the bottom of the influx
Lead TVD TVD at the top of the influx
Tail TVD TVD at the bottom of the influx
SBP Current surface backpressure
BHP Current bottomhole pressure
Influx Volume Current volume of the influx
Flow Out Flow rate of the fluids exiting the wellbore
Δ Pit Volume Amount that the volume of the fluid in the mud pit has changed
Flow In Flow rate of the fluids entering the wellbore at the pumps
Pressure at RCD Pressure at the rotating control device
Pressure at CS Pressure at the casing shoe
SPP Current standpipe pressure
