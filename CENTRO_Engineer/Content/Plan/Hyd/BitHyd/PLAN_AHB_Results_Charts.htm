Viewing the analysis chart

After you run A bit hydraulics analysis, the Calculate button () in the top toolbar will be replaced with the Show Graphs button (). This button is used to switch between the main view, which contains the analysis settings and the results summary, and the charts view.

To view the charts for A bit hydraulics experiment:

  1. Go to the Planning > Hydraulics > Bit Hydraulics Opt page.
  2. Select the experiment that you want to view results for.
  3. Click the Show Graphs button () in the top toolbar.

The charts will appear on top of the analysis settings. To close the analysis charts, click the Show Graphs button () again.

Viewing chart legends

By default, the chart legends are not shown. To pin the legends to the charts:

  1. Hover your mouse over the bottom right corner of the graph index, on the right side of the analysis charts.
  2. In the toolbar that appears, click the Show/Hide Legend button ().

The legend for each chart will appear in the top right corner of the graph.

Analysis chart

The analysis chart for hydraulics optimization shows the effects of flow rate on hydraulic horsepower and impact force. Force is plotted on the left axis, and horsepower is plotted on the right axis.

  • The blue dotted line on the left represents the minimum flow rate.
  • The orange dotted line on the right represents the maximum flow rate.
  • The brown line represents jet impact force.
  • The blue line represents pump hydraulic horsepower.
  • The green line represents bit hydraulic horsepower.
  • The red line represents hydraulic horsepower of the parasitic loss in the system. Parasitic loss is the system-wide pressure loss that occurs in the drillstring, annulus, and surface connections.
