Viewing friction factor charts

The analysis charts for torque and drag analysis also include friction factor charts. To view friction factor charts:

  1. Go to the Torque and Drag (T&D) page.
  2. Select the experiment you want to view results for.
  3. Click the Show Graphs button () in the top toolbar.
  4. Click the Show Friction Factor button ().

The friction factor charts will appear on top of the analysis charts. To close the friction factor charts and return to the analysis charts, click the Show Friction Factor button () again.

List of friction factor charts

The results for torque and drag analysis also include friction factor charts. The following charts are available when you are viewing friction factor charts:

Friction factor charts for a torque and drag analysis
Chart Name Description Chart Sample
Hook Load Shows the effect of different friction factors on the hookload at the surface when tripping in, tripping out, or rotating off-bottom.
Surface Torque (ROB) Shows the effect of different friction factors on surface torque when rotating off-bottom.
