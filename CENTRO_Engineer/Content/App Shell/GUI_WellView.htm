Well View pane

Knowledge Base
Related Topics

  • Incorrect BHP after reducing well depth

The Well View pane, on the right side of the screen, is visible from any screen in CENTRO® Engineer. It includes a visualization of the well and relevant information about the well, such as casing depths and sizes, mud weight, pressure, and bit position.

The Well View pane can also be hidden from the screen.

  • To hide the Well View pane, click the Minimize button () in the top right corner of the screen.
  • To restore the Well View pane, click the Maximize/Restore button ().

The visualization in the Well View pane varies, depending on the current screen. On the Dashboards screen, the Well View pane shows a complete, live preview of the well that includes the formation, well intervals, and work string. The Well View pane also includes fluid, casing, pressure, and depth information. You can change the appearance of the well preview by updating the current well scheme. See "Well schemes".

The preview also shows the active fluid in the wellbore, color-coded according to the fill color selected in the fluid properties. The intensity of the fluid color shown increases as the mud weight increases. See "Fluids".

The view on the Dashboards screen is the view that is shown throughout most of CENTRO® Engineer. Some of the pages on the Setup screen, however, use special views with a more limited preview of the well. On the Wells > Formations tab, for example, the Well View pane only shows the formation layers in the well. On the Surface Equipment > Equipment Configuration tab, the Well View pane shows a preview of the rig.
