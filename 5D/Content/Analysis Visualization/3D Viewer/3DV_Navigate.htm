About camera views

The 3D Viewer window has several camera views that allow you to explore the data in the field.

  • The Walk View allows you to view the field as if you were walking through a 3D model. See "Walk Viewer".
  • The Top View displays the field from above in plan view. See "Top Viewer".
  • The Side View displays the whole field from the side in two dimensions. See "Side Viewer".
  • The 2D Profile Plot option generates two-dimensional plots for the top view and the side view of the selected well. To generate profile plots, select a well in the Well List, then click the Walk Viewer button () and select the 2D Profile Plot option.
  • The All View option displays the Walk View, Top View, and Side View simultaneously.

To change the current view, click the Walk Viewer button () in the toolbar on the right side of the 3D Viewer window. You can also change the view by going to 3D Viewer > Set Viewer in the main menu.

Walk Viewer

The Walk View allows you to view the field as if you were walking through a 3D model.

  • To move the view forward through the field, drag the mouse towards the top of the screen.
  • To move the view backwards through the field, drag the mouse towards the bottom of the screen.
  • To rotate the view, drag the mouse to the left or to the right.
  • To fix the view in the current viewing pane, press the Shift key and drag the mouse in the desired direction.
  • To fix the view in the current position and move the camera in a horizontal direction, press the Alt key and drag the mouse to the left or right.
  • To adjust the angle of the camera, press the Ctrl key and drag the mouse in the desired direction.
  • To reset the camera angle, press the Space bar.

Top Viewer

The Top View displays the field from above in plan view. All movements are in the horizontal plane.

  • To zoom out, drag the mouse towards the top of the screen.
  • To zoom in, drag the mouse towards the bottom of the screen.
  • To reposition the field, press the Shift key and drag the mouse to the desired position.
  • To rotate the view around the center of the field, press the Ctrl + Shift keys and drag the mouse to the desired position.

Side Viewer

The Side View displays the whole field from the side in two dimensions. This allows you to see the field as it would be plotted on a flat sheet of paper along a specified viewing angle. All movements are in the vertical plane perpendicular to the chosen viewing angle.

  • To change the viewing angle, use the Central Rotation slider bar at the bottom of the Side Viewer window.
  • To zoom out, drag the mouse towards the top of the screen.
  • To zoom in, drag the mouse towards the bottom of the screen.
  • To reposition the field, press the Shift key and drag the mouse to the desired position.
  • To rotate the view around the center of the field, press the Ctrl + Shift keys and drag the mouse to the desired position.
