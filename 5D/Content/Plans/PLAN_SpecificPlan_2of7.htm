About 2 of 7 planning

2 of 7 planning creates a wellpath with five sections, using a hold-curve-hold-curve-hold geometry. You specify five of the seven possible parameters, and the remaining parameters are calculated to create a wellpath from the surface to the target.

This planning method is useful when you have specific constraints for your wellpath design but need to optimize certain parameters. For example, you might know your desired inclinations and dogleg severities but want to calculate the optimal kick-off point and hold section lengths.

The five sections in the wellpath are:

  • Vertical hold section from surface to kick-off point
  • First curved section that builds inclination
  • First hold section at constant inclination
  • Second curved section that adjusts inclination
  • Final hold section to the target

Before you can create a 2 of 7 plan, the 2 of 7 option must be enabled in the Options and Settings window. See "Enabling 2 of 7 planning". The well must also be associated with a target set that only contains one target. See "Target Sets".

Note

2 of 7 planning can only be used for wells with zero inclination at the surface.

Enabling 2 of 7 planning

To enable 2 of 7 planning:

  1. Click the Options and Settings button () in the main menu.
  2. Go to the Well Parameter Lists page.
  3. In the Specific Well Plans section, select the 2 of 7 option.
  4. Click OK to save your changes.

The 2 of 7 option will appear in the list of available options when you right-click on a well or go to Field Items > Well in the main menu. See "Creating a 2 of 7 plan".

Creating a 2 of 7 plan

To create a 2 of 7 plan:

  1. Right-click the well in the Data List pane, go to Specific Plan, then select the 2 of 7 option.
  2. In the 2 of 7 window, select two unknown values.
  3. Enter the remaining known values.
  4. Click OK to save your changes.

A wellpath will be created from the surface to the target depth, using the five known parameters specified in the settings. The values for the unknown parameters will be calculated when the plan is created.

Field definitions

The table below defines the fields in the 2 of 7 window.

Fields in the 2 of 7 window
Field Name Description
KOP TVD of the kick-off point
Build Rate 1 Dogleg severity for the first curved section
Inclination 1 Inclination at the end of the first curve / start of the first hold section
Hold Length 1 Length of the first hold section (Δ TVD)
Build Rate 2 Dogleg severity for the second curved section
Inclination 2 Inclination at the end of the second curve / start of the second hold section
Hold Length 2 Length of the second hold section (Δ TVD)
