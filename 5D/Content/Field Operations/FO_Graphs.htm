Using the Graph pane

About the Graph pane

The Graph pane contains charts that show different views of the wellpath, as well as diagnostic information about the wellpath. There are six options for plotting the well:

  • The Section View option shows the side view of the wellpath by plotting the vertical section against TVD. The current plan is shown in blue, and the definitive survey is shown in red.
  • The Top View option shows the top view of the wellpath by plotting the east offset against the north offset. The current plan is shown in blue, and the definitive survey is shown in red.
  • The Projected option shows the projected wellpath, including recovery sections and projection sections. See "Projection View".
  • The Trav. Cyl. option shows the travelling cylinder around the wellpath. See "Travelling Cylinder View".
  • The Qual. Ctrl. option contains charts for inclination, azimuth, dogleg severity, high side consistency, and lateral consistency. See "Quality Control View".
  • The Tortuosity option contains charts for tortuosity, absolute tortuosity, and directional difficulty index (DDI).
Tip

To update the azimuth of the vertical section, click the VS Azimuth button at the top of the Graph pane.

Measuring distances

Measurement mode allows you to measure the distance between two points on the Section View, Top View, or Projected charts.

To measure a distance on a chart:

  1. Select the chart that you measure distances for.
  2. Click the Start Measuring button ().
  3. Click on the first point in the chart.
  4. Click on the second point in the chart.
  5. To exit Measurement mode, click the Stop Measuring button ().

A line connecting to the two points will appear on the chart. On the Section View and Projected charts, the TVD and vertical section of each point will appear below the graph, as well as the distance and inclination between the two points. On the Top View graph, the north offset and east offset of each point will appear below the graph, as well as the distance and azimuth between the two points.

Using the Graph pane

The toolbar on the right side of the Graph pane is used customize the items shown on the charts. To zoom the selected chart, change the value in the Zoom field on the right side of the toolbar. To scroll to a different point in the chart, press the Alt key and drag the mouse to the desired location.
