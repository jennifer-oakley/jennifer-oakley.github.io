About the Well View pane

The Well View pane, on the right side of the screen, is visible from any screen in Victus™ Simulation. It has four primary areas:

  • The Rig State banner, which controls the current rig operation. See "Rig State banner".
  • The Utilities toolbar, which allows you to quickly switch fluids, view incoming data, take a screen capture, and access the user manual. See "Utilities".
  • The Well Preview panel, which includes a visualization of the well and relevant information about the well, such as casing depths and sizes, mud weight, pressure, and bit position. See "Well preview".
  • Control Tiles, which allow you to adjust chokes, pumps, MPD managed pressure drilling controls, pressure relief valves, and kick/loss detection. See "Control Tiles".

The Well View pane can also be hidden from the screen.

  • To hide the Well View pane, click the Minimize button () in the top right corner of the screen.
  • To restore the Well View pane, click the Maximize/Restore button ().

Rig State banner

The Rig State banner shows the current rig activity. When the activity on the rig changes, you'll also need to change the activity in Victus™ Simulation. The activities in Victus™ Simulation include various operations for drilling, tripping, casing, wireline, and other activities. You can also create custom rig operations on the Setup > Catalogs > Operations tab. See "Operations catalog".

To change the current rig activity:

  1. Click on the Rig State banner.
  2. Go to the appropriate category for the new rig activity.
  3. Select the current rig activity from the list that appears.

The Rig State banner will be updated to show the new right activity, and a note will be added to the timeline log.

Well preview

The visualization in the Well View pane varies, depending on the current screen. On the Home screen, the Well View pane shows a complete, live preview of the well that includes the formation, well intervals, and work string. The Well View pane also includes fluid, casing, pressure, and depth information. You can change the appearance of the well preview by updating the current well scheme. See "Well schemes".

The preview also shows the active fluid in the wellbore, color-coded according to the mud type selected in the fluid properties. The intensity of the fluid color shown increases as the mud weight increases. See "Fluid properties".

The view on the Home screen is the view that is shown throughout most of Victus™ Simulation. Some of the pages on the Setup screen, however, use special views with a more limited preview of the well. On the Well Data > Formations tab, for example, the Well View pane only shows the formation layers in the well. On the Surface Equipment > Equipment Configuration tab, the Well View pane shows a preview of the rig.
