Displacement summary

The results summary for a displacement analysis includes a schedule of the operations included in the analysis.

Each row in the table is color-coded according to the operation that is in progress at that point in the displacement. For example, values calculated during the first operation will have a red marker at the beginning of the row. Values calculated during the second operation will have a blue marker.

The results summary also includes the fluid positions for each of the different fluids. When you click on a row in the table, the Well View pane will be updated with the fluid positions at that point in the displacement. To see an animation of the fluids moving through the wellbore, select the first row in the table and press the Down arrow on the keyboard.

The analysis calculates the following values for the displacement:

Calculated values for displacement analysis
Column Definition
Time The amount of time elapsed since the start of the cementing operation
Fluid Depth Position of the fluid as its pumped through the well
Work String Depth Depth of the work string at the current time
Volume The total volume of fluid pumped into the well rate since the start of the cementing operation
Flow In Flow rate of the fluid flowing into the well
Flow Out Flow rate of the fluid flowing out of the well
SPP Standpipe pressure
Initial SBP Initial surface backpressure
Max SBP Maximum surface backpressure that can be set without exceeding the fracture gradient
Dynamic SBP Surface backpressure required to meet the pore pressure limit
SBP to Trap if Pumps Fail Surface backpressure required to maintain pressure above pore pressure, in the event of pump failure
BH P Bottomhole pressure
BH ECD Bottomhole equivalent circulating density
Final BH ECD Final bottomhole ECD, which accounts for dynamic SBP and bottomhole pressure
CS P Pressure at the last casing shoe
CS ECD ECD at the last casing shoe
Final CS ECD Final ECD at the last casing shoe, which accounts for dynamic SBP and casing shoe pressure
Ann Press @ High Limit Depth Annular pressure at the high limit depth specified in the advanced analysis settings
ECD @ High Limit Depth Equivalent circulating density at the high limit depth specified in the advanced analysis settings
Final ECD @ High Limit Depth Final equivalent circulating density at the high limit depth specified in the advanced analysis settings
Ann Press @ Target Depth Annular pressure at the target depth specified in the advanced analysis settings
ECD @ Target Depth Equivalent circulating density at the target depth specified in the advanced analysis settings
Final ECD @ Target Depth Final equivalent circulating density at the target depth specified in the advanced analysis settings
Ann Press @ DOI 1 Annular pressure at the first depth of interest specified in the advanced analysis settings. If the first depth of interest isn't specified, this value won't be calculated.
Pipe Press @ DOI 1 Pressure in the work string at the first depth of interest specified in the advanced analysis settings. If the first depth of interest isn't specified, this value won't be calculated.
ECD @ DOI 1 Equivalent circulating density at the first depth of interest specified in the advanced analysis settings. If the first depth of interest isn't specified, this value won't be calculated.
Ann Press @ DOI 2

Annular pressure at the second depth of interest specified in the advanced analysis settings. If the second depth of interest isn't specified, this value won't be calculated.
Pipe Press @ DOI 2 Pressure in the work string at the second depth of interest specified in the advanced analysis settings. If the second depth of interest isn't specified, this value won't be calculated.
ECD @ DOI 2 Equivalent circulating density at the second depth of interest specified in the advanced analysis settings. If the second depth of interest isn't specified, this value won't be calculated.
Fluid Weight Density of the fluid
Pipe/Annulus Location of the fluid
