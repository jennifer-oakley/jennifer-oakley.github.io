About hole sections

Open hole sections are listed in the bottom portion of the Well Intervals tab, and casing sections are listed at the top. Open hole sections are also listed in order, from the surface of the well to the bottom of the well. The top of the first open hole section is determined automatically, based on the rig elevations on the Surface Equipment page. See "Surface equipment".

Defining open hole sections

To add an open hole section:

  1. Go to the Well Intervals tab on the Setup > Well Data page.
  2. Select the numbered tab for the last wellbore instance in the well.
  3. Click the Insert Item button (), then select the OH option in the menu that appears.
  4. Double-click the MD field in the row that appears, then enter the depth at the bottom of open hole section.
  5. Enter the hole size in the Hole Diameter field.
  6. Enter the friction factor for the open hole section in the Friction Factor field.
  7. Click the Save Changes button () at the bottom of the screen.

The new open hole section will be added to the list of open hole sections in the bottom half of the screen. The dimensions of the new open hole section will be appear in the Well View pane.

Importing open hole sections

To import open hole sections from a CSV file:

  1. Go to the Well Intervals tab on the Setup > Well Data page.
  2. Use the tabs on the right side of the screen to select a well interval.
  3. Click the Import Open Hole button () at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Click Yes on the warning message that appears.
  5. Navigate to the file you want to import and click Open. A list of import options will appear in Victus™ Simulation.
  6. Select the options for the measured depth data in the file you are importing.
    • MD data is the name of the MD column.
    • MD unit is the measurement unit for MD.
  7. Select the options for the hole diameter in the import file.
    • Hole Diameter Data is the name of the hole diameter column.
    • Hole Diameter Unit is the measurement unit for the hole diameter.
  8. Select the options for the friction factor data, then click the OK button ().
    • Friction Factor data is the name of the friction factor column.
  9. When the import finishes, click the Save Changes button () at the bottom of the screen.

The new open hole section will be added to the list of open hole sections in the bottom half of the screen. The dimensions of the new open hole section will be appear in the Well View pane.

Field definitions

The table below defines the properties for open hole sections.

Properties of open hole sections
Field Definition
MD Measured depth at the bottom of the section
Hole Diameter Hole size of the section
Friction Factor Friction factor for the section
