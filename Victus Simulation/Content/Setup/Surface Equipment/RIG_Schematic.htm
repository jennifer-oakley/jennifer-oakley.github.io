About the rig schematic

The Setup > Surface Equipment > Piping and Instrumentation tab contains a schematic of the surface equipment and piping on the rig. The equipment that appears in the rig schematic depends on the type of rig, the location of the BOP, and the equipment selected on the Equipment Configuration tab.

Customizing surface pipe

To customize the surface pipe in the rig schematic:

  1. Select a pipe section in the rig schematic. The pipe section will be highlighted in orange.
  2. Click the Insert Item button () at the bottom of the screen. A new row will appear in the table in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. In the table, enter the length of the pipe section in the L column.
  4. In the ID column, enter the inside diameter of the pipe section.
  5. In the Type column, select the type of surface pipe used for the pipe section.
  6. In the # of 90° Elbows column, enter the number of elbows in the pipe section that have a 90 degree bend angle.
  7. In the # of 45° Elbows column, enter the number of elbows that have a 45 degree bend angle.

The rig schematic will be updated with the properties of the pipe. If you are using a custom rig size, the custom piping will be accounted for in surface pressure loss calculations.

Moving equipment

To move a piece of equipment to another location in the rig schematic:

  1. Select the equipment in the rig schematic. Orange, rotating arrows will appear around the selected piece of equipment.
  2. Click the Move Item button () at the bottom of the screen.
  3. In the rig schematic, click on the new location of the equipment.

The equipment will move to the location selected in the rig schematic. Any piping connected to the equipment will automatically be re-routed to the new location.

Linking equipment

To link together two pieces of equipment that are not connected:

  1. Select the first piece of equipment in the rig schematic.
  2. Click the Link Item button () at the bottom of the screen.
  3. On the rig schematic, select the equipment you want to connect it to.

New piping that connects the two pieces of equipment will appear on the schematic.

Adding sensors, flow meters, and valves

To add a sensor, flow meter, or valve:

  1. Click the Insert Item button () at the bottom of screen.
  2. Select the type of item you want to add.
  3. Drag the mouse cursor and drop the item on top of the piping where you want to add the item. The color of the piping will change to red when a valid location is selected.

The new item will appear in the rig schematic, in the middle of the selected section of piping.

Equipment views

Plan view, with rig pump filter applied

Front view, without a filter applied

Use the drop-down menus at the bottom of the screen to update the view shown on the Piping and Instrumentation tab. The first menu is used to highlight certain types of equipment on the rig schematic. For example, when the Rig Pump option is selected, all of the pumps are highlighted on the schematic with green arrows. The second menu is used to change the viewing angle.

  • The ISO option shows a three-dimensional schematic of the entire rig.
  • The Plan option shows a two-dimensional layout of the rig.
  • The Front option shows a three-dimensional view of the rig from the front.
  • The Side option shows a three-dimensional view of the rig from the side.

Using the Piping and Instrumentation tab

The toolbar on the Piping and Instrumentation tab is used to move equipment and link equipment together, as well as to add and remove sensors, flow meters, and valves.
