Licensing

About CENTRO® licensing

When you initially install CENTRO® Admin, a temporary license will be automatically generated. However, this license will expire seven days after installing CENTRO® Admin. To continue using CENTRO® Admin, you will need to obtain a permanent license from the CENTRO® Support Team.

To obtain a permanent license:

  1. Locate the temporary license information on the server. See "Locating the temporary license information".
  2. Email the license information to the CENTRO® Support Team at centro.help@weatherford.com.
  3. Update the license after you receive the new license information from the support team. See "Updating the license information".

Locating the temporary license information

To locate the temporary license ID:

  1. Log into the server that you need a license for.
  2. In your web browser, go to http://localhost:8200.
  3. On the sign-in screen, click the Username tab.
  4. Enter the following username and password, then click Sign In.
    • Username: Admin
    • Password: p@ssw0rd
  5. Click secret/, then click Default.
  6. Locate the row for LicenseInfo, then click the Show Secret button ().
  7. Copy all of the text that appears in the text box on the right.
  8. Paste the license information into an email, and send it to centro.help@weatherford.com.

Once the CENTRO® Support Team receives your license request, they will send you an email with the new license information. See "Updating the license information".

Updating the license information

To update the license information:

  1. Copy the entire block of text that you received from the CENTRO® Support Team.
  2. Log into the server that you need to update the license for.
  3. In your web browser, go to http://localhost:8200.
  4. On the sign-in screen, click the Username tab.
  5. Enter the following username and password, then click Sign In.
    • Username: Admin
    • Password: p@ssw0rd
  6. Click secret/, then click Default.
  7. Click the Edit Secret button in the top right corner.
  8. Locate the row for LicenseInfo, then click the Show Secret button ().
  9. Delete all the text in the text box that appears, and paste the text from the licensing email into the text box.
  10. Click the Save button at the bottom of the screen.

The license information will be updated on the server. The new license will be valid until the expiration date specified in the license information that you received from the CENTRO® Support Team.
