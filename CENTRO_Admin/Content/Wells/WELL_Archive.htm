Archiving a well

About the well archive

On the Wells > Well Archival page, you can archive a well that has been completed. The Well Archival page shows the list of wells that are ready to be archived or have already been archived.

When a well is archived, the well's database will be deleted from the data center and the data hub. In addition:

  • The well will no longer be listed on the Wells > Well Management page.
  • All documents for the well will be archived.
  • Alarms for the well will be disabled.
  • WITSML replicators for the well will be stopped.
  • WITS to WITSML routes will be disabled.
  • All calculations for the well will be stopped.

Before a well can be archived, the status of the well on the Well Management page must be changed to Completed. See "Well management".

Tip

Completed wells are not shown by default on the Well Management page. To view completed wells on the Well Management page, go to the Well Status field at the top of the table and select the Completed option.

Archiving a well

To archive a well:

  1. Go to the Wells > Well Management page on the data hub.
  2. If completed wells aren't shown, go to the Well Status field at the top of the table, and select the Completed option.
  3. Click the Edit button () in the row for the well you want to archive.
  4. In the Well Details window, go to the Status field and select the Archive Ready option.
  5. Click the Save button at the bottom of the window.
  6. Go to the Wells > Well Archival page on the data center.
  7. Click the Archive button () in the row for the well you want to archive.
  8. In the confirmation message that appears, click Yes to begin archiving the well.

The database for the well will be deleted from the data center and the data hub, and all documents in the well's document store will be archived. Any alarms, WITSML replicators, and WITS to WITSML routes will be disabled.
