About indicators

Indicators are used to display information such as RCD data, flow, density, choke control, wellbore trajectory, rig activity, on/off bottom position, and block height. Victus™ Operation has the following indicators:

  • The Flow Difference indicator () shows the difference between the flow into the well and the flow out of the well. Flow in is determined from the calculated flow from the pumps into the well, and flow out is determined from the flow measured by the Coriolis meter as the fluid exits the well.
  • The Flow Out Difference indicator () shows the difference between the outgoing flow rate measured by the Coriolis meter and the outgoing flow rate calculated by the hydraulics model.
  • The Density Out indicator () shows the difference between the fluid density measured by the Coriolis meter and the fluid density calculated by the hydraulics model.
  • The Choke indicator () shows the current positions of the chokes.
  • The P & I indicator () allows you to adjust the proportional gain of the controller on the MPD manifold. This indicator is only functional when you are using one of the pressure control modes to manage pressure. See "Pressure controls".
  • The Survey indicator () shows different projections of the wellbore trajectory.
  • The On/Off Bottom indicator () shows whether the bit is on-bottom or off-bottom.
  • The Block Height indicator () shows the current block position.
  • The Performance indicator () shows a breakdown of the rig activity. See "Performance reports".
  • The RCD indicator () displays sensor data received from the RCD.

Adding indicators to a dashboard

To add an indicator to a dashboard:

  1. On the Home screen, select the dashboard that you want to add an indicator to.
  2. Click the Edit Dashboard button () in the bottom left corner of the screen.
  3. In the Dashboard Editor panel that appears on the left, expand the Indicators heading.
  4. Click on the icon for the type of indicator you want to create, then drag it to a blank area on the dashboard.
  5. If you want the indicator to span multiple grid areas, click on the bottom right corner of the indicator and drag it into an adjacent blank area.

The selected indicator will appear on the dashboard. Some indicators have additional options available when you click the Menu button () in the top left corner of the indicator.

  • In the Survey indicator (), you can change the trajectory view.
  • In the Performance indicator (), you can change the legend options.

RCD sensor definitions

The table below defines the information shown in the RCD indicator.

Information in the RCD indicator
Field Name Definition
Mode

Indicates the current control mode on the HMI.

  • Local – The HMI is set to local mode, or RCD communication is not enabled. In Local mode, the RCD cannot be controlled by Victus™ Operation. Press Ctrl + F7 on the HMI to switch to remote mode.
  • Victus – The HMI is set to remote mode, and RCD communication is enabled. In Victus mode, the RCD can be controlled by Victus™ Operation. Press Ctrl + F8 on the HMI to switch to local mode.
Pump Indicates the current status of the pumps connected to the RCD
Bearing Latch

Indicates the current status of the bearing latch. When the bearing latch is enabled, the secondary latch is automatically disabled.

  • Open – The bearing latch is open.
  • Close – The bearing latch is closed.
  • Off – The bearing latch is disabled.
Secondary Latch

Indicates the current status of the secondary latch. When the secondary latch is enabled, the bearing latch is automatically disabled.

  • Open – The secondary latch is open.
  • Off – The secondary latch is disabled.
Active Wellbore Sensor Indicates the current source of the active wellbore sensor
Latch Position Sensor Indicates the current status of the latch proximity sensor, which detects the position of the latch assembly
Latch Position Sensor Current reading from the latch position sensor
Return Filter Differential Amount of clogging on the return filter
Supply Filter Differential Amount of clogging on the supply filter
Bearing Open Volume Volume of fluid in the open line for the bearing latch
Bearing Close Volume Volume of fluid in the close line for the bearing latch
Secondary Open Volume Volume of fluid in the open line for the secondary latch
Bearing Latch Open Pressure Current pressure reading from the open line for the bearing latch
Bearing Latch Close Pressure Current pressure reading from the close line for the bearing latch
Secondary Latch Open Pressure Current pressure reading from the open line for the secondary latch
Hydraulic Pressure Current pump pressure
Oil Tank Level Level of the oil in the hydraulic tank
Oil Tank Temperature Temperature of the oil in the hydraulic tank
