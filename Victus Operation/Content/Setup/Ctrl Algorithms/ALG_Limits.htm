About the limits algorithm

Maintaining control over wellbore pressure is critical to ensuring safe and efficient operations. The limits algorithm on the Setup > Control Algorithms > Limits tab is designed to provide options to specify how the high limit will be applied. The function of the high limit is to prevent the wellbore pressure from exceeding safe operational thresholds during MPD operations. This is crucial during scenarios where surface backpressure levels rise, such as when encountering unexpected pressure influxes or while controlling annular friction.

The Limits tab offers you the flexibility to customize Victus™ Operation's response when the surface backpressure approaches or exceeds the high limit. This customization ensures that the MPD system adapts effectively to varying conditions, maintaining operational safety and efficiency.

Warning

If any active chokes are switched to Manual mode when Victus™ Operation switches to SBP mode, the SBP setpoint will update, according to the current pressure. If any chokes are switched back to Auto mode, and the surface backpressure does not exceed the threshold selected in the System goes to SBP mode at field, the SBP setpoint will be set to the current pressure at the time the chokes were switched to Auto mode.

Field definitions

The table below defines the limit settings on the Setup > Control Algorithms > Limits tab.

Field definitions for the limits algorithm
Field Definition
System goes to SBP mode at

Determines the point at which Victus™ Operation switches to SBP mode based on a percentage of the high limit you select, as well as the maximum value of the low limit.

  • 90% of High Limit – When the surface backpressure reaches 90% of the high limit, Victus™ Operation will switch to SBP mode and attempt to maintain the pressure. If the low limit exceeds 90% of the high limit, the low limit will be set to 90% of the high limit.
  • 80% of High Limit – When the surface backpressure reaches 80% of the high limit, Victus™ Operation will switch to SBP mode and attempt to maintain the pressure. If the low limit exceeds 80% of the high limit, the low limit will be set to 80% of the high limit.
  • 70% of High Limit – When the surface backpressure reaches 70% of the high limit, Victus™ Operation will switch to SBP mode and attempt to maintain the pressure. If the low limit exceeds 70% of the high limit, the low limit will be set to 70% of the high limit.
98% High Limit Reaction

Defines what actions Victus™ Operation will carry out when pressure reaches 98% of the high limit.

  • Fully Open Chokes to 100% – If the surface backpressure reaches 98% of the high limit, all chokes will be switched to Auto mode and fully opened, even if the choke is disabled or in Manual mode. The MPD mode will be changed to None. If a choke is in calibration mode, it will not open when surface backpressure reaches 98% of the high limit. The choke will only open when it is out of calibration mode.
  • Go to Low Limit (based on Pore Pressure) - SBP mode – If the surface backpressure reaches 98% of the high limit, Victus™ Operation will switch to SBP mode and reduce the SBP set point to the low limit. Only chokes in Auto mode will respond to the new setpoint. Chokes that are disabled or in Manual mode will remain unchanged.
Minimum AP setpoint based on:

Defines how the minimum annular pressure setpoint is calculated.

  • Hydrostatic – The minimum annular pressure setpoint will be established based on the hydrostatic pressure exerted by the fluid inside the annular space.

  • Hydrostatic + Friction – The minimum annular pressure setpoint will be established based on the hydrostatic pressure exerted by the fluid inside the annular space as well as the pressure losses due to frictional forces between the fluid and the inner surface of the pipes or casings.

  • Low Limit – The minimum annular pressure setpoint will be established based on the low limit value.
Override high limit during kick auto-control When this option is enabled, the firmware will override the high limit when a kick occurs and Auto Control is active in the Control Algorithms > Kick tab. See "Kick control".
High limit during kick control Sets the high limit to be used during a kick when the Override high limit during kick auto-control option is enabled. This high limit value will remain in use after the kick is controlled.
