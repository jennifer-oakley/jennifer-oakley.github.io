About well intervals

The Well Intervals tab on the Setup > Well Data page is used to define the geometry of the hole sections along the trajectory of the well. Casing sections are listed in the top portion of the Well Intervals tab, and open hole sections are listed at the bottom. The Well View pane shows a preview of the wellbore, including the casing sections, liner sections, and open hole sections.

There are two steps for defining the well intervals.

  • Define the open hole sections. See "Open hole sections".
  • Define the casing and liner sections. See "Casing and liner sections".

Using the Well Intervals tab

There are several keyboard shortcuts and mouse actions that you can use on the Setup > Well Data > Well Intervals tab.

  • To export well interval data to a CSV file, press the Ctrl + S keys.
  • To move a hole section to a different position in the wellbore, click on the section you want to move and drag it to the new position.
  • To edit a hole section, double-click on the section name in the list of wellbore sections. You can also press the Alt key and click on the section name.

The toolbar at the bottom of the screen on the Well Intervals tab is used add and remove sections in the wellbore.
