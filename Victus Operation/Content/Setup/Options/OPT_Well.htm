About well schemes

Well schemes control the visualization of the well in the Well View pane, as well as the information that is displayed. There are three default well schemes included in Victus™ Operation:

  • The Sketch well scheme shows only basic illustrations of the well intervals and work string, using a minimum amount of detail. It also displays information about the wellbore pressures, ECD, mud weight, casing shoes, and bit depth.
  • The Diagram well scheme shows realistic illustrations of the formation, well intervals, and work string. It also displays information about wellbore pressures and ECD.
  • The Engineering well scheme shows color-coded illustrations of the formation, well intervals, and work string. It also displays information about wellbore pressures and ECD.

Default well schemes in Victus™ Operation

Well scheme settings are stored on the Setup > Options page, under the Well Schemes tab. Each well scheme includes visualization options for the formation, well intervals, work string, and fluid. Well schemes also include settings for the information that appears in the Well View pane, such as casing depths, bit depth, mud weight, and pressure.

You can change the settings for the default well schemes, as well as create custom well schemes. Custom well schemes are stored in the Victus™ Operation database, but they are not unique to a particular well. If you create a new project, the settings for your well schemes will not be affected.

Selecting a well scheme

To change the current well scheme:

  1. Hover your mouse over the bottom right corner of the Well View pane.
  2. In the panel that appears, click the Well Scheme button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Click on the name of the well scheme you want to use.

The selected well scheme will automatically load in the Well View pane.

Adding a custom well scheme

To add a custom well scheme:

  1. Go to the Well Schemes tab on the Setup > Options page.
  2. Click the Insert Item button () at the bottom of the screen.
  3. In the new row that appears, go to the Scheme field and enter a name for the well scheme.
  4. Select the fill options you want to use in the Formation, Casing, Work String, and Fluid fields.
  5. Select the information that you want to include in the Casing Info, Well Info, Pressure Info, and Fluid Info fields.
  6. Click the Save Changes button () to save your changes.

The new well scheme will be added to the Victus™ Operation database. It will also be added to the list of available well schemes in the Well View pane. See "Selecting a well scheme".
