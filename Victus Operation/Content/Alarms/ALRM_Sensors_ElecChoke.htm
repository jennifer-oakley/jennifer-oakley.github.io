About electric choke alarms

The Alarms > Sensors > Electric Choke tab shows information about each choke and the status of several alarms for electric chokes on the MPD manifold. Color boxes are used to indicate the current status of each alarm.

  • A green box indicates that the choke is functioning as expected.
  • A red box indicates that the choke is not functioning as expected.

Alarm definitions

The table below defines the statuses and alarms shown on the Alarms > Sensors > Electric Choke tab.

  • Stop – The drive is stopped or holding zero speed.

  • Supply Loss – Supply loss condition has been detected.
  • Deceleration – The motor is being decelerated to zero speed because the final drive run has been deactivated.
  • DC Injection – The drive is applying DC injection braking.
  • Position – Position control is active during an orientation stop.
  • Trip – The drive has tripped and no longer controlling the motor.

Fire Mode Active Fire mode is enabled and active on the drive

Using the Electric Choke tab

The toolbar at the bottom of the screen on the Electric Choke tab is used to reset the drives for the chokes, as well as deactivate and active the choke drives.

Toolbar buttons the Alarms > Sensors > Electric Choke tab
Button Function
Resets the drive for the selected choke
Activates or deactivates the drive for the selected choke
